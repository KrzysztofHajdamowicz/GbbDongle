GbbDongle

GbbOptimizer ↔ Modbus RTU proxy for the Waveshare ESP32-S3-RS485-CAN

Connect the board over USB and click Install to flash the latest firmware straight from your browser (Chrome or Edge required).

After flashing

  1. Join the GbbDongle Setup WiFi access point and configure your WiFi.
  2. Open the device's web page (its IP address, shown in the captive portal).
  3. Fill in MQTT Server, Plant Id and Plant Token from your GbbOptimizer account.
  4. Press Apply Settings (Restart).
  5. Wire RS485 A/B to your inverter's Modbus port.
