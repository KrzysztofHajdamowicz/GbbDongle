GbbDongle

GbbOptimizer ↔ Modbus RTU proxy for off-the-shelf ESP32 RS485 boards

Pick your board, connect it over USB and click Install to flash the latest firmware straight from your browser (Chrome or Edge required).

ESP32 · Ethernet + PoE (no WiFi) · KAmodRPi UART RS485 ISO HAT (isolated, auto-direction)

Flash over USB with the RS485 HAT removed — the USB converter and the HAT share the same UART pins.

After flashing

WiFi boards (Waveshare, LilyGo):

  1. Join the GbbDongle Setup WiFi access point and configure your WiFi.
  2. Open the device's web page (its IP address, shown in the captive portal).

Kamami (Ethernet-only): unplug USB, mount the RS485 HAT, plug in Ethernet (PoE or 5 V) — the device gets an address via DHCP; open http://gbbdongle-kamami.local or its IP address from your router.

Then, on the device's web page:

  1. Fill in MQTT Server, Plant Id and Plant Token from your GbbOptimizer account.
  2. Press Apply Settings (Restart).
  3. Wire RS485 A/B to your inverter's Modbus port.
